What is Telekinesis?

Telekinesis is the ability to move objects with your mind. But There are also several types of telekinetic powers such as ;precognition (knowing future events before they happened);pyrokinesis (creating fire with the mind) telepathy (describing things at a remote location). The idea of moving things with mind power has intrigued people for centuries. Although in the late 1800s it has been seen as an ability that could be scientifically demonstrated. This occurred during the heyday of early spiritualism, when psychics claimed to contact the dead during seances. Objects would suddenly and mysteriously start to move, float, or fly by across the room, while seemingly being untouched by human hands.
